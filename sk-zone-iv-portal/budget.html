Budget Overview

The People's Budget Portal provides a simplified overview of the approved Annual Barangay Youth Investment Program (ABYIP) and Annual Budget of the Sangguniang Kabataan of Barangay Zone IV for Calendar Year 2025.

This page promotes transparency, accountability, and public participation by presenting how the SK Fund is allocated among the eleven (11) youth development program areas while providing access to official supporting documents.

Every allocation presented in this portal is based on the officially approved Annual Budget and related government documents.

Frequently Asked Questions

Learn more about youth planning, budgeting, and public financial information.

The SK Fund is the financial resource allocated for Sangguniang Kabataan operations and approved youth development programs, projects, and activities.

The Annual Barangay Youth Investment Program, or ABYIP, identifies the priority youth programs, projects, and activities intended for implementation during the fiscal year.

The annual budget is prepared based on approved youth development priorities and the corresponding investment program. Proposed appropriations are reviewed and approved through the appropriate Sangguniang Kabataan budget process.

Available public budget documents may be accessed through this transparency portal. Additional records may also be requested from the Sangguniang Kabataan of Barangay Zone IV through its official communication channels.
